About Denizli

Denizli, located in southwestern Turkey, is a province blessed with natural wonders, ancient history, and warm hospitality. Home to the world-famous Pamukkale thermal springs and the ancient city of Hierapolis, Denizli offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and historical treasures.

The name "Pamukkale" translates to "Cotton Castle" in Turkish, referring to the stunning white travertine terraces formed by mineral-rich thermal waters flowing down the mountainside. This UNESCO World Heritage site attracts millions of visitors each year who come to witness its otherworldly beauty.
